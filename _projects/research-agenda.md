We focus on Data-Centric Intelligent Systems, studying how data drives the capabilities and behaviors of intelligent systems while ensuring their reliability and security in real-world scenarios.

## Data Construction

We study methods for building high-quality data, including the synthesis, representation, filtering, and evaluation of heterogeneous data, with the goal of improving the capability and generalization of LLMs and agentic systems.

## Trustworthy Systems

We study system robustness and security mechanisms, focusing on model security, adversarial attacks and defenses, user behavior modeling, and anomaly detection to improve system reliability in real environments.

## Scenario-Driven Applications

We build secure and reliable intelligent system solutions for complex scenarios such as smart healthcare, cybersecurity, and social media.
